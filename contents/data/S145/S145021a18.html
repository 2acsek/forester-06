FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4DOTC) > Fuel Cut Valve

REMOVAL

WARNING:

Place “NO OPEN FLAMES” signs near the working area.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to spill fuel.

1. Remove the fuel tank.

2. Loosen the clip and disconnect the evaporation hose from the fuel cut valve.

3. Remove the bolts which install the fuel cut valve.
