SEATS > Front Seat

DISASSEMBLY

1. DRIVER’S SEAT

1. Remove the seat from vehicle.

2. Remove the reclining lever cover and reclining hinge cover.

3. Loosen the screws to remove hinge cover.

4. Remove the two bolts from the reclining hinge on each left and right side.

5. Remove the hook at bottom of seat backrest.

6. Open the fastener at the rear side of backrest.

7. Remove the hog rings (A) on the front side of backrest from the backrest frame. (Seat with side airbag)

8. Remove the hog ring (A) which secures the backrest cover and backrest pad.

9. Remove the headrest bushing.

NOTE:

Push outside to remove it from the inside of seat.

10. Pull out the backrest frame.

11. Remove the lower hog rings (A) on the front side of backrest.

12. Remove the hog rings (A) on the front side of backrest, and then remove the backrest cover from backrest.

13. Remove the connecting wire, then loosen the bolt and nuts to remove slide rail.

14. Remove the inner seat belt assembly.

15. Remove the hooks (A), and then remove the seat cushion frame.

16. Remove the hog rings (A), and then remove the seat cushion cover from seat cushion pad.

17. Open the fastener on the bottom of the headrest, then remove the headrest cover from the headrest.
