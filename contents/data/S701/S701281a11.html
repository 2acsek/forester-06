HVAC SYSTEM (HEATER, VENTILATOR AND A/C) > Compressor

INSTALLATION

1. Install in the reverse order of removal.

2. Replace the O-rings on low-/high-pressure hoses with new parts, then apply compressor oil.

3. After replacing the compressor, adjust the amount of compressor oil.

4. Charge refrigerant.

Refer to “COMPONENT” of “General Description”.
