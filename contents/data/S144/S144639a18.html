INTAKE (INDUCTION)(H4DOTC) > Resonator Chamber

REMOVAL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Remove the collector cover.

3. Remove the air intake duct.

4. Remove the air cleaner lower case.

5. Remove the resonator chamber mounting bolt on the right of engine compartment.

6. Remove the front tire RH, and lift the vehicle.

7. Remove the front mudguard RH.

8. Remove the resonator chamber from the inside front fender.
