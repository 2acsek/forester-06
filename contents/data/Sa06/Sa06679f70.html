IDENTIFICATION

1. IDENTIFICATION NUMBER & LABEL LOCATIONS

The V.I.N. (Vehicle Identification Numbers) is used to classify the vehicle.

• POSITIONING OF THE PLATE LABEL FOR IDENTIFICATION

• AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION

(1)

• MANUAL TRANSMISSION

(1)

(2)

MT type label
