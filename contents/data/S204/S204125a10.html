WHEEL AND TIRE SYSTEM > Tire

INSPECTION

1. Take stones, glass, nails etc. out of the tread groove.

2. Replace the tire.

CAUTION:

When replacing a tire, make sure to use only tires of the same size, construction and load range as originally installed.

(1) If large cracks on side wall, damage or cracks on the tread is found.

(2) When the “tread wear indicator” appears as a solid band across the tread.

(3) If a crack is found on the tire valve, replace the valve.
