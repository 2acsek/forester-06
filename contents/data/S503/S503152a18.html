MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL(5MT) > Front Differential Assembly

REMOVAL

1. Remove the manual transmission assembly from the vehicle.

2. Remove the transfer case together with the extension case assembly.

3. Remove the transmission case.

4. Remove the drive pinion shaft assembly.

5. Remove the main shaft assembly.

Single-range model

Dual range model

6. Remove the differential assembly.

NOTE:

• Do not confuse the right and left roller bearing outer races.

• Be careful not to damage the oil seal of retainer.

7. Remove the differential side retainers using ST.

ST    18630AA010 WRENCH COMPL RETAINER

8. Remove the bearing outer race from the transmission case.

ST    398527700 PULLER ASSY
