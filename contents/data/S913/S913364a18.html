EXTERIOR BODY PANELS > Rear Door

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from battery.

2. Remove the rear door trim.

3. Remove the rear sealing cover.

4. Remove the rear door glass.

5. Remove the rear door regulator and motor.

6. Remove the rear door latch.

7. Remove the rear outer handle.

8. Remove the rubber duct (A) from center pillar, and disconnect the connector of the door harness.

9. Put a wooden block on jack and place jack under the door. Support the door with a jack to protect it from damage.

NOTE:

When supporting the door with jack, work carefully so that you do not deform the hinge portion of door during work.

10. Remove the checker bolts.

11. Remove the door-side bolts for upper and lower hinges to remove the rear door panel.

12. Remove the attaching bolts (on the body side) for the upper and lower hinges using the ST to remove the door hinges.
