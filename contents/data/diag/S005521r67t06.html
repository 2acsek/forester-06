CHECK VDCCM MOTOR DRIVE TERMINAL.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

2) Remove the VDC connector cover.

3) Connect all connectors.

4) Install the motor relay.

5) Check the ABS sequence control.

6) Measure the voltage between VDCCM connector terminals.
