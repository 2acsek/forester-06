VEHICLE DYNAMICS CONTROL (VDC) > Steering Angle Sensor

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the airbag module.

WARNING:

Always refer to “Airbag System” before performing service on the airbag modules.

3. Remove the steering wheel nut, then pull the steering wheel from the shaft using a steering puller.

NOTE:

Always remove the steering wheel with it positioned straight-ahead.

(1)

Steering puller

4. Remove the screw securing the steering column lower cover.

5. Remove the two screws securing the steering column upper cover.

6. Unlock the harness band and disconnect the connector of the steering angle sensor.

(1)

Harness band

(2)

Connector

7. Remove the bolt which secure the roll connector and steering angle sensor to the steering column.

8. Remove the roll connector and steering angle sensor.

NOTE:

Do not rotate the steering angle sensor because the center position will be recognized by the VDCCM.
