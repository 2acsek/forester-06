VEHICLE DYNAMICS CONTROL (VDC) (DIAGNOSTICS) > General Description

CAUTION

1. SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEM “AIRBAG”

The airbag system wiring harness is routed near the ABS wheel speed sensor VDCCM and VDCH/U.

CAUTION:

• All airbag system wiring harnesses and connectors are yellow. Do not use the electrical test equipment on these circuits.

• Be careful not to damage the airbag system wiring harness when servicing the ABS wheel speed sensor, VDCCM and VDCH/U.
