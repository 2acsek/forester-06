Specification

Engine Load

1.0 — 3.2 (%): idling

4.0 — 9.6 (%): 2,500 rpm racing

Measuring condition:

• After engine is warmed-up.

• Gear position is in neutral.

• Turn the A/C to OFF.

• Turn all the accessory switches to OFF.
