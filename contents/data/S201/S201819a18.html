REAR SUSPENSION > Rear Trailing Link

REMOVAL

1. Loosen the rear wheel nuts.

2. Lift up the vehicle, then remove the rear wheels.

3. Remove both the rear parking brake clamp and the ABS wheel speed sensor harness.

4. Remove the bolt which attaches the trailing link to the trailing link bracket.
