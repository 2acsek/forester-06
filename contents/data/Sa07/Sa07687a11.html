INSTALLATION

1. NON-TURBO MODEL

Install in the reverse order of removal.

CAUTION:

Position the protrusion on the air cleaner lower case with the hole in the air cleaner upper cover to mount the upper cover on the case.

2. TURBO MODEL

Install in the reverse order of removal.

CAUTION:

Position the protrusion on the air cleaner upper cover with the hole in the air cleaner lower case to mount the upper cover on the case.
