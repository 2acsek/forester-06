AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION(4AT) > Inhibitor Switch

ADJUSTMENT

1. Shift the select lever to “N” range.

2. Loosen the three inhibitor switch assembly securing bolts.

3. Insert the ST as vertical as possible into the holes in the inhibitor switch lever and switch body.

ST    499267300 STOPPER PIN

4. Tighten the three inhibitor switch assembly securing bolts.

Tightening torque:

3.4 N·m (0.35 kgf-m, 2.6 ft-lb)

5. Repeat the inspections of the inhibitor switch. If the inhibitor switch is determined to be “faulty”, replace it.
