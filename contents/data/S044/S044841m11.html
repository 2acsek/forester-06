AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION (DIAGNOSTICS)(4AT)(D) > Diagnostic Procedure without Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC)

CHECK SPORT SHIFT SWITCH

DIAGNOSIS:

Input signal circuit of SPORT shift switch is open or shorted.

TROUBLE SYMPTOM:

Does not shift on sport shift mode.

WIRING DIAGRAM:

STEPCHECKYESNO

1.CHECK SPORT SHIFT SWITCH.

1) Move the select lever to sport shift mode.

2) Shift and hold the select lever to up side.

Does the LED illuminate?

2.CHECK SPORT SHIFT SWITCH.

Shift and hold the select lever to down side.

Does the LED illuminate?

3.CHECK STEERING SHIFT SWITCH.

Hold to press the + side of steering shift switch.

Does the LED illuminate?

4.CHECK STEERING SHIFT SWITCH.

Hold to press the − side of steering shift switch.

Does the LED illuminate?

Go to the procedure “CHECK SPORT SHIFT INDICATOR LIGHT”.

5.CHECK SPORT SHIFT SWITCH GROUND CIRCUIT.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

2) Disconnect the connector from SPORT shift switch.

3) Measure the resistance of harness between SPORT shift switch connector and chassis ground.

Connector & terminal

LHD

(B237) No. 2 — Chassis ground:

RHD

(B237) No. 6 — Chassis ground:

Is the resistance less than 1 Ω?

Repair the open circuit of harness between SPORT shift switch and chassis ground.

6.CHECK SPORT SHIFT SWITCH.

Measure the resistance between SPORT shift switch terminals.

Terminal

No. 6 — No. 2:

Is the resistance 1 MΩ or more?

Replace the guide plate assembly.

7.CHECK SPORT SHIFT SWITCH.

1) Move the select lever to sport shift mode.

2) Measure the resistance between the sport shift switch terminals while holding the select lever to the shift up side.

Terminal

No. 6 — No. 2:

Is the resistance less than 1 Ω?

Replace the guide plate assembly.

8.CHECK HARNESS CONNECTOR BETWEEN TCM AND SPORT SHIFT SWITCH.

1) Disconnect the connector from TCM.

2) Measure the resistance of harness between TCM connector and sport shift switch connector.

Connector & terminal

LHD

(B237) No. 6 — (B54) No. 19:

RHD

(B237) No. 2 — (B54) No. 19:

Is the resistance less than 1 Ω?

Repair the open circuit of harness between SPORT shift switch connector and TCM connector, or poor contact in connector.

10.CHECK INPUT SIGNAL TO TCM.

2) Turn the ignition switch to ON (engine OFF).

3) Measure the voltage of signal to TCM.

Connector & terminal

(B54) No. 19 (+) — Chassis ground (−):

Is the voltage 9 V or more?

Replace the TCM.
