FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4DO) > Engine Control Module (ECM)

INSTALLATION

Install in the reverse order of removal.

NOTE:

When replacing the ECM, be careful not to use ECM with incorrect specification to avoid any damage on the fuel injection system.

Tightening torque:

7.5 N·m (0.76 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)
