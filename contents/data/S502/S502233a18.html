AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION(4AT) > Transmission Mounting System

REMOVAL

1. PITCHING STOPPER

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the air intake chamber. (Non-turbo model)

3. Remove the intercooler. (Turbo model)

4. Remove the pitching stopper.

2. TRANSMISSION REAR CROSSMEMBER AND REAR CUSHION RUBBER

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Jack up the vehicle and support it with rigid racks.

3. Remove the front, center, rear exhaust pipes and muffler. (Non-turbo model)

4. Remove the center and rear exhaust pipe and muffler. (Turbo model)

5. Set the transmission jack under transmission.

NOTE:

Make sure that the support plate of transmission jack does not touch the oil pan.

6. Remove the transmission rear crossmember.

7. Remove the rear cushion rubber.
