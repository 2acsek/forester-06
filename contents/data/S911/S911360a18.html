EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIM > Roof Trim

REMOVAL

CAUTION:

When removing the clip, use great care not to damage the roof trim.

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the overhead console. (Model with sunroof)

3. Remove the room light.

4. Remove the sun visor and hook on both sides.

5. Remove the assist grip cap, and remove the assist grip (A).

6. Remove the upper inner trim.

7. Remove the rear quarter upper trim shown in the figure.

(1) Remove the rear rail trim (A).

(2) Remove the rear quarter upper trim (B) of both sides.

(C)

Clip

8. Remove the clips, and then remove the roof trim.
