EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIM > Front Hood Grille

INSTALLATION

(A)

Black clip

(B)

Black clip

(C)

Bolt

1. Replace the black clips (1) and (2) with new parts. Install the clip with arrow mark facing the front side of grille (A).

2. Replace the black clips (3) with a new part.

3. Insert the front hood grille clip and engage the three clips.

NOTE:

Make sure that the anchor portion of each clip is firmly engaged.

4. Install the five nuts.

Tightening torque:

4.4 N·m (0.45 kgf-m, 3.25 ft-lb)

5. Hook the two hooks (A) of grille duct to the hood holes.

6. Install the grille duct with the five screws and clip (A).

CAUTION:

When the grommet installed to the hood inner is broken, replace the grommet with new part.

Tightening torque:

1.0 N·m (0.1 kgf-m, 0.74 ft-lb)
