SUNROOF/T-TOP/CONVERTIBLE TOP (SUNROOF) > Sunroof Motor

ADJUSTMENT

1. Reset the sunroof motor.

(1) Completely close the sunroof lid. At this time, it operates intermittently in 50 mm (1.97 in) amounts. The switch must be pressed several times.

(2) Then continue pushing the switch for more than one second to the CLOSE side.

(3) Open the sunroof lid approximately 400 mm (15.75 in) out.

(4) Completely close the sunroof lid, then continue pushing the switch for more than one second to the CLOSE side. (This operation enables the auto operation.)
