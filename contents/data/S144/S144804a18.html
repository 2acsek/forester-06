INTAKE (INDUCTION)(H4DOTC) > Intercooler

REMOVAL

1. Remove the collector cover.

2. Disconnect the PCV hose from PCV pipe.

3. Remove the air by-pass valve from intercooler.

4. Remove the bolts which secure the intercooler to the bracket.

5. Loosen the clamps (A) which connect the turbocharger and intercooler duct.

6. Loosen the clamps (B) which connect the throttle body and intercooler.

7. Separate the intercooler duct from turbocharger.

8. Remove the intercooler from throttle body.
