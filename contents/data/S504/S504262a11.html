CLUTCH SYSTEM > Clutch Pipe and Hose

INSTALLATION

1. ALL MODELS EXCLUDING THE RHD TURBO MODEL

Install in the reverse order of removal.

Tightening torque:

T1: 15 N·m (1.5 kgf-m, 10.8 ft-lb)

T2: 18 N·m (1.8 kgf-m, 13.0 ft-lb)

T3: 25 N·m (2.5 kgf-m, 18.1 ft-lb)

(A)

Clutch pipe (LHD model)

(B)

Clip

(C)

Clutch hose

(D)

Clutch pipe (RHD model)

2. RHD TURBO MODEL

Install in the reverse order of removal.

Tightening torque:

T1: 7.5 N·m (0.8 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)

T2: 7.8 N·m (0.8 kgf-m, 5.8 ft-lb)

T3: 15 N·m (1.5 kgf-m, 10.8 ft-lb)

T4: 18 N·m (1.8 kgf-m, 13.0 ft-lb)

T5: 25 N·m (2.5 kgf-m, 18.1 ft-lb)

(A)

Clutch pipe

(B)

Connector

(C)

Clutch hose
