MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL(5MT) > Switches and Harness

REMOVAL

1. BACK-UP LIGHT SWITCH & NEUTRAL POSITION SWITCH

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the air intake duct and air cleaner case. (Non-turbo model)

3. Remove the intercooler. (Turbo model)

4. Disconnect the connector back-up light switch & neutral position switch.

• Non-turbo model

(A)

Neutral position switch (Brown)

(B)

Back-up light switch (Gray)

(C)

High-low switch (Black)

• Turbo model

5. Lift the vehicle.

6. Remove the back-up light switch & neutral position switch with the harness.

(A)

Neutral position switch (Brown)

(B)

Back-up light switch (Gray)

2. HIGH-LOW SWITCH

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the air intake duct and cleaner case.

3. Disconnect the high-low switch connectors.

(A)

Neutral position switch (Brown)

(B)

Back-up light switch (Gray)

(C)

High-low switch (Black)

4. Remove the high-low switch cable from the clamp.

5. Remove the high-low switch.

Clamp

(B)
