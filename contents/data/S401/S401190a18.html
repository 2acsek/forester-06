ABS > Front ABS Wheel Speed Sensor

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from battery.

2. Disconnect the ABS wheel speed sensor connector located next to the front strut mounting house in the engine compartment.

3. Remove the bolts which secures the ABS wheel speed sensor harness to the strut.

4. Remove front height sensor harness clip from the bracket (models with HID, right side only), and remove the bolt which holds the ABS wheel speed sensor harness to the body.

(3)

5. Remove the bolts which secure front ABS wheel speed sensor to the housing, and remove the front ABS wheel speed sensor.

CAUTION:

• Be careful not to damage the pole piece and the face of the teeth located at the end of the ABS wheel speed sensor during removal.

• Do not pull on the ABS wheel speed sensor harness during removal.
