IGNITION(H4DO) > Spark Plug

REMOVAL

Spark plug:

1. RH SIDE

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the collector cover. (Turbo model)

3. Remove the air cleaner case.

4. Disconnect the connector from ignition coil.

5. Remove the ignition coil.

6. Remove the spark plug with a spark plug socket.

2. LH SIDE

1. Disconnect the ground cable from battery and remove the battery and battery carrier.

2. Remove the collector cover. (Turbo model)

3. Remove the secondary air pump.

4. Disconnect the connector from ignition coil.

5. Remove the ignition coil.

6. Remove the spark plug with a spark plug socket.
