VEHICLE DYNAMICS CONTROL (VDC) > VDC Sequence Control

SPECIFICATION

1. ENDING CONDITIONS FOR VDC SEQUENCE CONTROL

When the following conditions develop, the VDC sequence control stops and VDC operation is returned to the normal mode.

1. When the speed of at least one wheel reaches 10 km/h (6 MPH).

2. When the brake pedal is pressed during sequence control and the stop light switch is set to ON

3. After completion of the sequence control.

4. When a problem is detected
