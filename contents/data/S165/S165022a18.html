FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4DO) > Fuel Filler Pipe

REMOVAL

WARNING:

Place “NO OPEN FLAMES” signs near the working area.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to spill fuel.

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

3. Open the fuel filler flap lid, and remove the fuel filler cap.

4. Remove the screws which secure gasket.

5. Remove the rear wheel RH.

6. Lift the vehicle.

7. Drain fuel from fuel tank.

8. Remove the fuel filler pipe protector.

9. Disconnect the evaporation hose from the clip of fuel filler pipe.

10. Disconnect the air vent hose from the fuel filler pipe.

11. Remove the bolts which hold fuel filler pipe bracket on the body.

12. Loosen the clamp and disconnect the fuel filler hose (A) from the fuel filler pipe.

13. Move the clips, and disconnect the air vent hose (B).

14. Remove the fuel filler pipe from the underside of the vehicle.

15. Remove the air vent pipe together with the clips from the body.
