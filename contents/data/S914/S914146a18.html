LIGHTING SYSTEM > Front Height Sensor

REMOVAL

CAUTION:

Before removing the suspension related parts, be sure to remove the vehicle height sensor.

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Lift-up the vehicle and remove the right front wheel.

3. Disconnect the sensor connector.

4. Remove the bracket from front crossmember.

5. Remove the bracket from transverse link.

CAUTION:

Do not disassemble the sensor and lever.
