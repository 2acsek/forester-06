CHECK HARNESS BETWEEN TUMBLE GENERATOR VALVE POSITION SENSOR CONNECTOR AND ECM CONNECTOR.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

2) Disconnect the connector from tumble generator valve position sensor.

3) Measure the resistance of harness between tumble generator valve position sensor connector and engine ground.

Connector & terminal
