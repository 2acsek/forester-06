CHECK FRONT VEHICLE SPEED SENSOR.

1) Lift up the vehicle and support with rigid racks.

2) Start the engine.

3) Shift the select lever to “D” range and slowly increase vehicle speed.

NOTE:

The speed difference between front and rear wheels may illuminate the ABS warning light, but this indicates no malfunction. When AT control diagnosis is finished, perform the ABS or VDC clear memory of on-board diagnostics system.
