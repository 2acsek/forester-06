BRAKE > Brake Pedal

INSTALLATION

1. Install in the reverse order of removal.

CAUTION:

• If the cable clamp is damaged, replace it with a new part.

• Be sure to cover outer cable end with boot.

• Be careful not to twist the accelerator cable.

• Always use a new clevis pin.

2. Adjust the clutch pedal.

3. Check the brake pedal after installation.
