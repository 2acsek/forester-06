POWER ASSISTED SYSTEM (POWER STEERING) > Tilt Steering Column

REMOVAL

(1)

Tilt steering column

(2)

Universal joint

Tightening torque:N·m (kgf-m, ft-lb)

       

T1:

24 (2.4, 17.7)

       

T2:

25 (2.5, 18.1)

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

3. Remove the airbag module.

WARNING:

Always refer to “Airbag System” before performing service on the airbag modules.

4. Remove the steering wheel.

5. Remove the universal joint.

6. Remove the trim panel under instrument panel.

7. Remove the steering column lower cover.

8. Remove all connectors from the steering column.

9. Remove the two bolts under instrument panel securing the steering column.

10. Pull out the steering shaft assembly from the hole on toe board.

CAUTION:

• Be sure to remove the universal joint before removing steering shaft assembly installing bolt, when removing the steering shaft assembly or when lowering it to service other parts.

• Do not loosen the tilt lever when the steering column is not secured to the vehicle.
