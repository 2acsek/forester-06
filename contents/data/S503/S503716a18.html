MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL(5MT) > Counter Gear

REMOVAL

1. Remove the manual transmission assembly from the vehicle.

2. Remove the transfer case together with the extension case assembly.

3. Remove the transmission case.

4. Move the counter shaft until it reaches the transmission case, and remove the snap ring with appropriate tools.

(A)

Snap ring

5. Slide the washer to the rear of the high low counter shaft, and remove the straight pin from the counter shaft.

(A)

Straight pin

(B)

Counter gear

6. Being careful not to drop the counter gear and 2 washers, remove the counter shaft from the transmission case.

NOTE:

• Be careful not to damage the O-ring.

• Be careful not to drop the front side straight pin.

• Be careful not to drop the collar included with the 2 needle bearings and the counter gear.
