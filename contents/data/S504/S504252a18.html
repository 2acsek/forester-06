CLUTCH SYSTEM > Clutch Disc and Cover

REMOVAL

1. Remove the transmission assembly from vehicle body.

2. Attach the ST on the flywheel.

ST    499747100 CRANKSHAFT STOPPER

(A)

Clutch cover

3. Remove the clutch cover and clutch disc.

NOTE:

• Take care not to allow oil to touch the clutch disc face.

• Do not disassemble the clutch cover or clutch disc.
