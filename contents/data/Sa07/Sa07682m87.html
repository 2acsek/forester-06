A/C filter

Perform the inspection in every 12 months or 12,000 km (7,500 miles).

Note (4)

Symbols used:

R: Replace

I: Inspection

(I): Recommended service for safe vehicle operation.

NOTE:

2. ATF filter is maintenance free part. ATF filter needs replacement when it is physically damaged or ATF leaked.

3. When towing is performed, replace the rear differential oil within 24,000 km (15,000 miles). (KA model)

4. When the A/C filter is installed.
