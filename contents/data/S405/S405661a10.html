INSPECTION

Inspect the following items for the removed parts, and replace those that apply.

1. If the PHV cable boot is damaged or deteriorated, or if the inner cable is damaged or rusted.

2. Check for weak return springs, damage, and rust.

3. Check that the rotation of the ball can be heard when the PHV is tilted and the lever is moved smoothly.

CAUTION:

The PHV cannot be disassembled. If it requires replacement, replace the entire PHV assembly.
