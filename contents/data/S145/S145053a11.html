FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4DOTC) > Fuel Tank

INSTALLATION

1. Support the fuel tank with transmission jack and push the fuel tank harness into access hole with grommet.

2. Set the fuel tank and temporarily tighten the bolts of fuel tank bands.

WARNING:

A helper is required to perform this work.

3. Connect the two-way valve hose (A) to the two-way valve, and connect the canister hose (B) to the canister.

4. Connect the fuel filler flap lid hose and air vent hose.

5. Connect the fuel hoses, and secure with clips and quick connectors.

6. Tighten the band mounting bolts.

Tightening torque:

33 N·m (3.4 kgf-m, 25 ft-lb)

7. Install the rear crossmember.

8. Connect connector (A) to the fuel tank cord and plug the service hole with grommet (B).

9. Set the rear seat and floor mats.

10. Connect the connector to the fuel pump relay.

11. Install the passenger’s side front side sill cover.

12. Install the glove box.

13. Connect the battery ground cable to the battery.

14. Inspect the wheel alignment and adjust if necessary.

15. Re-initialize the auto headlight beam leveler system.
