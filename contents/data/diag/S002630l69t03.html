CHECK AIRBAG MAIN HARNESS.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF, disconnect the battery ground cable, and wait for 20 seconds or more.

2) Disconnect the two airbag resistors to the connectors (2P) and (3P) in the test harness P.

3) Remove the instrument panel lower cover, and disconnect the connectors (AB21) and (AB26).

4) Disconnect the connector (AB3) from airbag control module, and connect the test harness R to connector (1R).

5) Measure resistance between connector (2R) in the test harness R and connector (3P) in the test harness P.
