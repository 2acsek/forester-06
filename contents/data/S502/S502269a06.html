AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION(4AT) > Drive Pinion Shaft Assembly

DISASSEMBLY

1. Flatten the lock nut tab, and then remove the lock nut while holding the rear spline part of the drive pinion shaft using ST1 and ST2. Pull out the drive pinion collar.

ST1    498937110 HOLDER
ST2    499787700 WRENCH
ST3    499787500 ADAPTER

2. Remove the O-ring.

3. Separate the roller bearing and outer race from the drive pinion shaft using a press.

(A)

Outer race

4. Separate the front roller bearing from the drive pinion shaft using a press and the ST.

ST    498517000 REPLACER

(A)

Front roller bearing
