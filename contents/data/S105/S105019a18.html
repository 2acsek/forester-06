FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4SO) > Fuel Delivery, Return and Evaporation Lines

REMOVAL

CAUTION:

• Be careful not to spill fuel.

• Catch the fuel from hoses using a container or cloth.

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Release the fuel pressure.

3. Open the fuel filler flap lid, and remove the fuel filler cap.

4. Remove the floor mat.

5. Disconnect the fuel delivery tube and quick connectors, and then disconnect the fuel return pipes and hoses, evaporation pipes and hoses.

6. In the engine room, disconnect fuel delivery hose (A), return hose (B) and evaporation hose (C).

CAUTION:

• Be careful not to spill fuel.

• Catch the fuel from hoses using a container or cloth.

7. Lift up the vehicle.

8. Disconnect the two-way valve hose (A) from the two-way valve, and disconnect the canister hose (B) from the canister.

9. Disconnect the quick connector on the fuel line.

(1) Clean the pipe and connector, if they are covered with dust.

(2) To prevent from damaging or entering foreign matter, wrap the pipes and connectors with plastic bag etc.

(3) Hold the connector (A) and push the retainer (B) down.

(4) Pull out the connector (A) from the retainer (B).
