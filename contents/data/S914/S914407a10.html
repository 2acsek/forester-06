LIGHTING SYSTEM > Side Turn Signal Light Assembly

INSPECTION

1. Install the side turn signal light assembly and check that it blinks normally.

2. If it does not blink normally, replace the side turn signal light assembly with a new part.

NOTE:

Since a LED (Light Emitting Diode) is used for the door mirror built-in type side turn signal light, replace the side turn signal light assembly if the LED does not turn on.
