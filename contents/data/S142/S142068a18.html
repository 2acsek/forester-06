EXHAUST(H4DOTC) > Rear Exhaust Pipe

REMOVAL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

3. Lift up the vehicle.

4. Separate the rear exhaust pipe from center exhaust pipe.

CAUTION:

Be careful, exhaust pipe is hot.

5. Separate the rear exhaust pipe from muffler.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to pull down the rear exhaust pipe.

6. Apply a coat of spray type lubricant to the mating area of cushion rubber.

7. Remove the rear exhaust pipe from cushion rubber.
