HVAC SYSTEM (HEATER, VENTILATOR AND A/C) > Evaporator

REMOVAL

1. Recover the coolant and disconnect the toe board A/C pipe.

2. Remove the glove box.

3. Remove the blower motor unit assembly.

4. Loosen the screws to remove evaporator cover.

5. Remove the auto A/C hose in the unit.

6. Rotate the evaporator core top portion to the front of the vehicle and the bottom portion to lay over into the compartment, then pull out the heater case to the left side.

7. Loosen the two bolts to remove the expansion valve.

CAUTION:

If the evaporator is replaced, add an appropriate amount of compressor oil to evaporator.
