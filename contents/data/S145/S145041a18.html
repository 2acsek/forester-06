FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4DOTC) > Camshaft Position Sensor

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the collector cover.

3. Disconnect the connector from camshaft position sensor RH.

4. Remove the camshaft position sensor RH from the cylinder head rear.

5. Remove the cam shaft position sensor LH in the same way as RH.
