Be careful not to damage the oil seal of the extension.

1. Extract the circlip with ST1, ST2 and ST3 and the press.

ST1    398673600 COMPRESSOR
ST2    498627100 SEAT
ST3    398663600 PLIERS

2. Remove the test plug.

Extension case

(B)

Test plug

3. Remove the clutch piston using compressed air.

4. Remove the transfer clutch pipe without bending the pipe.

5. Remove the dust cover from extension case.

6. Remove the oil seal from the extension case.
