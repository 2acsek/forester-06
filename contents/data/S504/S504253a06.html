CLUTCH SYSTEM > Operating Cylinder

DISASSEMBLY

1. Remove the boot and push rod.

2. Apply compressed air from the clutch hose installation hole.

NOTE:

To prevent the piston from jumping out, position the piston hole downward, and place materials such as a wooden block.

3. Separate the piston and piston spring.
