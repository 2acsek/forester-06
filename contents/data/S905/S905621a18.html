GLASS/WINDOWS/MIRRORS > Power Window Control Switch

REMOVAL

1. MAIN SWITCH

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the screw cover by using a flat tip screwdriver.

3. Loosen the screw to remove power window main switch.

4. Disconnect the connector.

2. SUB-SWITCH

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the screw securing the switch panel.

3. Remove the switch panel.

4. Disconnect the connector.
