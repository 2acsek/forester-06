POWER ASSISTED SYSTEM (POWER STEERING) > Steering Wheel

INSPECTION

1. Check the steering wheel for deformation. If the deformation is excessive, replace the steering wheel.

2. Check the splines on the steering wheel for damage. If the damage is excessive, replace the steering wheel.

3. If the steering wheel spokes are not horizontal when wheels are set in the straight ahead position, or error is more than 5° on the periphery of the steering wheel, re-install the steering wheel correctly.

(1)

Within 5°
