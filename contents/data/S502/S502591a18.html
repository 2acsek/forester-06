AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION(4AT) > Reduction Drive Gear

REMOVAL

1. Remove the transmission assembly from vehicle body.

2. Remove the rear wheel speed sensor, and separate the extension case from transmission case.

3. Remove the reduction driven gear.

4. Using the ST, extract the reduction drive gear assembly.
