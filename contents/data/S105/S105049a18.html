FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4SO) > Engine Control Module (ECM)

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the side sill front cover on the passenger’s side.

3. Detach the floor mat of passenger’s seat.

4. Remove the protect cover.

5. Remove the bolts and nuts which hold the ECM to the bracket.

6. Remove the clip (A) from bracket.

7. Disconnect the ECM connectors, and take out the ECM.
