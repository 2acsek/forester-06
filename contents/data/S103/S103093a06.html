MECHANICAL(H4SO) > Cylinder Head

DISASSEMBLY

1. Place the cylinder head on the ST.

ST    498267800 CYLINDER HEAD TABLE

2. Set the ST on valve spring. Compress the valve spring and remove the valve spring retainer key. Remove each valve and valve spring.

ST    499718000 VALVE SPRING REMOVER

NOTE:

• Mark each valve to prevent confusion.

• Be careful not to damage rip of intake valve oil seal and the exhaust valve oil seal.

• Keep all the removed parts in order for re-installing in their original positions.
