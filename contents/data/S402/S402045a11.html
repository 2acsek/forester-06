VEHICLE DYNAMICS CONTROL (VDC) > VDC Control Module (VDCCM)

INSTALLATION

Install in the reverse order of removal.

CAUTION:

After installation, always make the following two settings.

• SRS roll connector and steering angle sensor centering setting

• Yaw rate & lateral G sensor 0 point setting

These two procedures are required to make the VDCCM recognize what position the vehicle is in later. Refer to “Adjustments” for procedures for the settings above.
