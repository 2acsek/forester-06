REAR SUSPENSION > Rear Crossmember

INSTALLATION

1. Install in the reverse order of removal.

NOTE:

• Use a new self-locking nut.

• Tighten the nut with the bolt head secured when installing the adjusting bolt.

2. Rear differential attachment and tightening torque;

or

3. When attaching the rear vehicle height sensor bracket to the lateral link and rear crossmember, tighten to the following torque values.

Tightening torque

7.5 N·m (0.8 kgf-m, 5.5 ft-lb)

4. Always tighten bushings with wheels in full contact with the ground and the vehicle at curb weight.

5. Inspect the wheel alignment and adjust if necessary.

6. Re-initialize the auto headlight beam leveler system. (Model with HID)
