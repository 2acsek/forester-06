CHECK HARNESS BETWEEN IGNITION SWITCH ILLUMINATION AND KEYLESS ENTRY CONTROL MODULE (MODEL WITHOUT DOUBLE LOCK) OR DOUBLE LOCK UNIT (MODEL WITH DOUBLE LOCK).

1) Disconnect the keyless entry control module or double lock unit harness connector.

2) Measure the resistance between the keyless entry control module or double lock unit harness connector terminal and ignition switch illumination harness connector.
