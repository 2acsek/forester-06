CHECK PASSENGER’S AIRBAG MODULE.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF, disconnect the battery ground cable, and wait for 20 seconds or more.

2) Pull out two stopper pins and lower the glove box.

3) Disconnect the connectors (AB27) and (AB4) of the passenger’s airbag module.

4) Connect the connector (1P) in the test harness P to connector (AB4).

5) Connect the two airbag resistors to the connectors (2P) and (3P) in the test harness P.

6) Connect the battery ground terminal and turn the ignition switch to ON.
