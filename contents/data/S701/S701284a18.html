HVAC SYSTEM (HEATER, VENTILATOR AND A/C) > Air Vent Grille

REMOVAL

1. CENTER GRILLE

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the center console panel.

3. Loosen two screws to remove the center air vent grille.

2. SIDE GRILLE

Remove by prying the side bearing grille in four places RH and LH.
