EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIM > Roof Rail

REMOVAL

1. Remove the roof trim.

2. Remove the five installing nuts.

3. Carefully remove the clip on the front end in the horizontal direction, then remove the roof rail in the upward direction.

NOTE:

When removing the C clip, apply force to it in the horizontal direction and then remove in the upward direction while bending the a section side claw out of the way.
