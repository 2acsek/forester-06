POWER ASSISTED SYSTEM (POWER STEERING) > Steering Wheel

INSTALLATION

WARNING:

Always refer to “Airbag System” before performing service on the airbag modules.

1. Align the center position of the roll connector.

2. Install in the reverse order of removal.

NOTE:

Align the alignment marks on the steering wheel and steering shaft.

Tightening torque:

45 N·m (4.6 kgf-m, 33 ft-lb)

Column cover to steering wheel clearance

2 — 4 mm (0.08 — 0.16 in)

CAUTION:

Insert the roll connector guide pin into the guide hole on the lower end of the steering wheel surface. Avoid damaging the pin.
