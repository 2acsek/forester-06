LUBRICATION(H4SO) > Engine Oil Cooler

REMOVAL

1. DOHC NON-TURBO MODEL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Lift up the vehicle.

3. Remove the under cover.

4. Drain the engine oil.

5. Drain engine coolant.

6. Remove the water by-pass pipe between oil cooler and water pump.

7. Remove the engine oil filter.

8. Remove the oil cooler connector and remove the oil cooler.

2. TURBO MODEL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Lift up the vehicle.

3. Remove the under cover.

4. Drain the engine oil.

5. Drain engine coolant.

6. Remove the water by-pass pipe between oil cooler and water pump.

7. Remove the engine oil filter.

8. Remove the connector and remove the oil cooler.

9. Remove the adapters (1) and (2).
