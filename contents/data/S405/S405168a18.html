BRAKE > Master Cylinder

REMOVAL

CAUTION:

Do not let brake fluid come into contact with the painted surface of the vehicle body. Completely wash away with water immediately and wipe off if it was accidental.

1. Drain brake fluid from reservoir tank completely.

2. Disconnect the harness connector of the fluid level gauge.

3. Remove the brake pipes from master cylinder.

4. Remove the master cylinder mounting nuts, and remove the master cylinder from the brake booster.
