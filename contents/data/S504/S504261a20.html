CLUTCH SYSTEM > Clutch Fluid

REPLACEMENT

CAUTION:

• Use FMVSS No. 116, new DOT3 or DOT4 brake fluid.

• Cover the bleeder with cloth to prevent brake fluid from being splashed on surrounding parts when loosening the bleeder.

• Avoid mixing different brands of brake fluid to prevent the quality of fluid from degrading.

• Be careful not to allow dirt or dust to get into the reservoir tank.

NOTE:

For ease and safety, perform work with 2 persons.

1. Remove the air cleaner case and air duct.

2. Jack-up the vehicle and place a rigid rack under it, or raise the vehicle on a lift.

3. Drain the brake fluid from the reservoir tank.

4. Refill the reservoir tank with new recommended brake fluid.

Recommended brake fluid:

FMVSS No. 116, new DOT3 or DOT4 brake fluid

5. Bleed air from the clutch fluid, if necessary.
