CHECK OUTPUT SIGNAL FOR AT SHIFT LOCK CONTROL MODULE.

1) Disconnect all connectors.

2) Turn the ignition switch to ON. (engine OFF)

3) Move the select lever to “P” range.

4) Depress the brake pedal.

5) Measure the voltage between the AT shift lock control module connector and chassis ground.
