AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION(4AT) > Transmission Mounting System

INSPECTION

Perform the following inspection procedures and repair or replace defective parts.

1. PITCHING STOPPER

Make sure that the pitching stopper is not bent or damaged. Check that there are no cracks, hardening or damage on rubber parts.

2. TRANSMISSION REAR CROSSMEMBER AND REAR CUSHION RUBBER

Make sure that the cross member is not bent or damaged. Check the cushion rubber for cracks, hardening or damage.
