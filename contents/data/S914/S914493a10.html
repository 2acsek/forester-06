LIGHTING SYSTEM > Headlight Beam Leveler System

INSPECTION

1. HEADLIGHT BEAM LEVELER ACTUATOR

1. Turn on the headlight.

2. Operate the switch in the order of 0 → 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5, and check that the headlight beam position is lowered.

2. HEADLIGHT BEAM LEVELER SWITCH

Connect the circuit tester to the headlight beam leveler switch connector. Measure the resistance at each switch position.
