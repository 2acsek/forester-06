MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL(5MT) > Main Shaft Assembly for Dual-range

DISASSEMBLY

1. Wrap vinyl tape around the main shaft spline to protect the oil seal from damage. Then pull out the oil seal and needle bearing by hand.

2. Flatten the tab of the axle nut.

3. Remove the lock nut from transmission main shaft assembly.

ST1    498937000 TRANSMISSION HOLDER
ST2    499987003 SOCKET WRENCH (35)

4. Remove the 5th hub & sleeve No. 2, baulk lever, baulk ring, 5th drive gear and needle bearing.

NOTE:

If necessary, use the new sleeve gear & hub assembly, when replacing the sleeve or hub assembly. Because these must engage at the specified point, do not disassemble. If it must be disassembled, mark the engaging point on the spline beforehand.
