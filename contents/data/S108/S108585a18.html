LUBRICATION(H4SO) > Engine Oil Filter

REMOVAL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Lift up the vehicle.

3. Remove the 6 service hole clips on top of the under cover.

4. Rotate the service hole cover in the direction of the arrow.

5. Remove the oil filter using ST.

ST    18332AA000 OIL FILTER WRENCH (OUTER DIAMETER: 68 MM (2.68 IN) FOR OIL FILTER)
ST    18332AA010 OIL FILTER WRENCH (OUTER DIAMETER: 65 MM (2.56 IN) FOR OIL FILTER)

(A)

Drain plug

(B)

Oil filter
