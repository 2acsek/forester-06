BRAKE > Front Brake Pad

INSPECTION

Check the pad thickness A.

Pad thickness

Standard

11 mm (0.43 in)

Wear limit

1.5 mm (0.059 in)

NOTE:

• Always replace the pads of both sides as a set.

• Replace pad clips if they are twisted or worn.

• There is a wear indicator on the RH side inner pad. If the pad is worn to the limit, the end of wear indicator contacts disc rotor, and a squeaking sound is heard as the wheel rotates. If the sound is heard, replace the pad.

• Replace the pad if there is oil or grease on it.
