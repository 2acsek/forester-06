REMOVAL

1. Remove the air intake chamber. (Non-turbo model)

2. Remove the intercooler. (Turbo model)

3. Remove the clutch hose from operating cylinder.

NOTE:

Cover the hose joint to prevent the clutch fluid from flowing out.
