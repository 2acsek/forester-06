REMOVAL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Remove the gear shift knob.

3. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

4. Remove the console box.

5. Remove the clamp.

6. Remove the bushing & insulator.

7. Remove the plate COMPL from vehicle body.

8. Lift the vehicle.

9. Remove the rear exhaust pipe and muffler.

Turbo model
