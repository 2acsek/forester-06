SEATS > Rear Seat

DISASSEMBLY

1. Remove the rear seat.

2. Remove the hog rings (A) from the vicinity of the seat backrest.

3. Remove the hog rings (A) on the front side of cushion pad, then remove the cover.

4. Remove the hog rings (A) from the vicinity of the seat cushion.

5. Remove the hog rings (A), and then remove the cover.
