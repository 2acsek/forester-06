INTAKE (INDUCTION)(H4DOTC) > Air Cleaner Case

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from battery.

2. Disconnect the connector from mass air flow and intake air temperature sensor.

3. Loosen the clamps (A) which connect the air intake boot and the intake duct.

4. Remove the clip (B) from air cleaner upper cover.

5. Remove the air cleaner upper cover.

6. Remove the air cleaner element.

7. Remove the air cleaner lower case.
