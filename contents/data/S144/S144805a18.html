INTAKE (INDUCTION)(H4DOTC) > Turbocharger

REMOVAL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Disconnect the ground cable from battery.

3. Remove the collector cover.

4. Remove the center exhaust pipe.

5. Lower the vehicle.

6. Separate the turbocharger joint pipe from turbocharger.

7. Disconnect the engine coolant hose which is connected to coolant filler tank.

8. Loosen the clamp which secures turbocharger to intake duct.

9. Disconnect the engine coolant hose from pipe and remove the turbocharger bracket RH.

10. Remove the oil inlet pipe from turbocharger.

(A)

Oil inlet pipe

(B)

Metal gasket

11. Disconnect the oil outlet hose from pipe.

12. Take out the turbocharger from engine compartment.
