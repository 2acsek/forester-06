CHECK SIDE AIRBAG HARNESS (LH).

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF, disconnect the battery ground cable, and wait for 20 seconds or more.

2) Disconnect the connector (AB19) from the seat belt pretensioner (LH).

3) Disconnect the airbag resistor from test harness.

4) Disconnect the connector (AB7) from airbag control module.

5) Connect connector (1R) in the test harness R to the connector (AB7).

6) Connect the battery ground terminal and turn the ignition switch to ON.

7) Measure the voltage between connector (3R) in the test harness R and chassis ground.
