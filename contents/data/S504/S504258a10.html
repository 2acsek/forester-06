CLUTCH SYSTEM > Clutch Switch

INSPECTION

Check the clutch switch for continuity. If the resistance is not at the standard value, replace the switch.

(1) Disconnect the clutch switch connector.

(2) Measure the resistance between terminal 1 and 2 of the switch.
