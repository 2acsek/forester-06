EXHAUST(H4SO) > Muffler

INSTALLATION

Install in the reverse order of removal.

NOTE:

• Use a new gasket and self-locking nut.

• When the lubricant was applied to the cushion rubber during the removal/installation, degrease it after reassembling.

Tightening torque:

48 N·m (4.9 kgf-m, 35.4 ft-lb)
