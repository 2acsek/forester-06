AIRBAG SYSTEM > Passenger’s Airbag Module

REMOVAL

CAUTION:

Refer to “CAUTION” of “General Description” before handling the airbag module.

1. Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

2. Disconnect the ground cable from battery and wait for at least 20 seconds before starting work.

3. Remove the glove box.

4. Disconnect the airbag connector.

5. Remove the three bolts, and then carefully remove the airbag module.

6. Refer to “CAUTION” for handling of a removed airbag module.
