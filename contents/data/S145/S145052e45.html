FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4DOTC) > Fuel

PROCEDURE

1. RELEASING OF FUEL PRESSURE

WARNING:

Place “NO OPEN FLAMES” signs near the working area.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to spill fuel.

1. Remove the glove box.

2. Remove the side sill cover of passenger’s side.

3. Disconnect the connector from fuel pump relay.

4. Start the engine and run it until it stalls.

5. After the engine stalls, crank it for five more seconds.

6. Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

2. DRAINING FUEL

WARNING:

Place “NO OPEN FLAMES” signs near the working area.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to spill fuel.

1. Release the fuel pressure.

2. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

3. Remove the fuel pump.

4. Drain fuel using a pump through where the fuel pump was installed.

CAUTION:

Use a petrol resistance pump or the equivalent.

5. Install the fuel pump.
