DRIVE SHAFT SYSTEM > Front Drive Shaft

DISASSEMBLY

1. Place alignment marks on the shaft and outer race.

2. Remove the AARi boot band and boot.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to damage the boot.

3. Remove the AARi outer race from the shaft assembly.

4. Wipe off grease.

CAUTION:

This grease is a special type of grease. Do not mix it with other grease.

5. Place alignment marks on the trunnion and shaft.

6. Remove the snap ring and trunnion.

CAUTION:

Be sure to wrap shaft splines with vinyl tape to protect the boot from scratches.

7. Remove the AARi boot.

8. Place the drive shaft between wooden blocks and secure it with a vise.

CAUTION:

Do not set the drive shaft on a vise directly. Be sure to use wooden blocks.

9. Lift the boot band tabs using a screwdriver and hammer.

(A)

Boot band tab

10. Disconnect the boot and remove it.

11. Hit the inner race of AC joint to remove AC joint from the shaft.
