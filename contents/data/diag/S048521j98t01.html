CHECK HARNESS BETWEEN ECM AND OIL FLOW CONTROL SOLENOID VALVE.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

2) Disconnect the connectors from ECM and oil flow control solenoid valve.

3) Measure the resistance between ECM and oil flow control solenoid valve.
