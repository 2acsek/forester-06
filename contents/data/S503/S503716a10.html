MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL(5MT) > Counter Gear

INSPECTION

1. After installing the snap ring, measure the clearance between the snap ring and counter washer.

Clearance:

0.05 — 0.35 mm (0.0020 — 0.0138 in)

2. If the clearance is not within the measurement standard, select an appropriate snap ring to adjust the clearance to be within the standard.
