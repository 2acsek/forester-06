CHECK REAR OXYGEN SENSOR.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

2) Measure the resistance between the rear oxygen sensor connector terminals.

Terminals

No. 1 — No. 2:

QUESTION

Is the resistance less than 30 Ω?

Yes No

Repair the harness and connector.

NOTE:

In this case, repair the following item:

• Open circuit of harness between the rear oxygen sensor and ECM connector

• Poor contact of the rear oxygen sensor connector

• Poor contact in ECM connector

• Poor contact of coupling connector

Replace the rear oxygen sensor.
