SUNROOF/T-TOP/CONVERTIBLE TOP (SUNROOF) > Sunroof Assembly

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the roof trim.

3. Remove the sunroof lid.

4. Disconnect the four drain tubes (A) from the sunroof frame.

5. Disconnect the sunroof harness connector.

6. Remove the bolts and then remove the frame bracket.

7. Remove the nuts and bolts to remove the sunroof frame.
