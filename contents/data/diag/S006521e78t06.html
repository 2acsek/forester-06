CHECK ABS WHEEL SPEED SENSOR GAP.

Measure the gap between the ABS wheel speed sensor protrusion and tone wheel.

QUESTION

Is the gap within the following? Front wheel: 0.3 — 0.8 mm (0.012 — 0.031 in); Rear wheel: 0.7 — 1.2 mm (0.028 — 0.047 in)

Yes No

Adjust the gap.

NOTE:

Adjust the gap using spacers (Part No. 26755AA000). If spacers cannot correct the gap, replace the worn sensor or worn tone wheel.
