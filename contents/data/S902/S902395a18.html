WIPER AND WASHER SYSTEMS > Combination Switch (Wiper)

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the instrument panel lower cover.

3. Loosen the screw to remove a steering column lower cover.

4. Remove the steering column upper cover mounting screw.

5. Disconnect the connector from the combination switch.

6. Loosen the screw to remove the combination switch.
