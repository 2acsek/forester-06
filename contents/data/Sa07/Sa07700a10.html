PERIODIC MAINTENANCE SERVICES > Tire Rotation

INSPECTION

1. If the depth of tread is less than 1.6 mm (0.063 in), or the tread wear indicator has appeared across the tire tread, replace the tire.

CAUTION:

Replace the left and right sides at the same time.

2. If unevenly worn, adjust the alignment.

3. Rotate tires periodically (12,500 km (7,500 miles)) as shown in the figure, in order to prevent uneven wear and to prolong life.
