EXHAUST(H4SO) > Rear Exhaust Pipe

REMOVAL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Lift up the vehicle.

3. Separate the rear exhaust pipe from center exhaust pipe.

4. Separate the rear exhaust pipe from muffler.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to pull down the rear exhaust pipe.

5. Apply a coat of spray type lubricant to the mating area of cushion rubber.

6. Remove the rear exhaust pipe bracket from cushion rubber.
