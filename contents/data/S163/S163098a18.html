MECHANICAL(H4DO) > Crank Pulley

REMOVAL

NOTE:

If only a single part is replaced, perform the work without removing the engine from vehicle.

1. Remove the V-belts.

2. Remove the crank pulley bolt. To lock the crankshaft, use ST.

ST    499977400 CRANK PULLEY WRENCH (DOHC NON-TURBO MODEL)
ST    499977100 CRANK PULLEY WRENCH (DOHC TURBO MODEL)

3. Remove the crank pulley.
