VEHICLE DYNAMICS CONTROL (VDC) > VDC Hydraulic Control Module (VDCH/U)

INSTALLATION

1. VDCH/U

1. Install the VDCH/U.

Tightening torque:

18 N·m (1.8 kgf-m, 13.0 ft-lb)

2. Secure the ground wire of the VDCH/U and apply the specified grease to the ground terminals.

Specified grease:

Nippeco LT or GB

Tightening torque:

33 N·m (3.4 kgf-m, 25 ft-lb)

3. Connect brake pipes to the VDCH/U connectors.

Tightening torque:

15 N·m (1.5 kgf-m, 10.6 ft-lb)

4. Fasten the VDCH/U connector to the connector bracket.

CAUTION:

Match the connector to the corresponding socket.

5. Connect the connector to VDCH/U.

(1)

Relay box connector

(2)

VDCH/U connector

6. Install the air intake duct.

7. Connect the battery ground cable to the battery.

8. Bleed air from the brake system.

2. RELAY BOX

1. Install the relay box and connector bracket.

Tightening torque:

13 N·m (1.3 kgf-m, 9.4 ft-lb)

2. Secure the relay box connector to the connector bracket.

CAUTION:

Match the connector to the corresponding socket.

3. Connect the connector to the relay box.

(1)

Relay box connector

(2)

VDCH/U connector

4. Install the air intake duct.

5. Connect the battery ground cable to the battery.
