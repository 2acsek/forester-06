LUBRICATION(H4SO) > Oil Pressure Switch

INSTALLATION

1. Apply liquid gasket to the oil pressure switch threads.

Liquid gasket:

THREE BOND 1324 (Part No. 004403042) or equivalent

2. Install the oil pressure switch onto engine block.

Tightening torque:

25 N·m (2.5 kgf-m, 18.1 ft-lb)

3. Connect the terminal to the oil pressure switch.

4. Install the generator to bracket.

5. Install the collector cover. (Turbo model)
