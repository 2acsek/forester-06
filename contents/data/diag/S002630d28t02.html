CHECK SIDE AIRBAG MODULE.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF, disconnect the battery ground cable, and wait for 20 seconds or more.

2) Disconnect the connector (AB11) from the side airbag module (RH), and connect the connector (1F) in test harness F to connector (AB11).

3) Connect the airbag resistor to the connector (3F) of test harness F.

4) Connect the battery ground terminal and turn the ignition switch to ON.
