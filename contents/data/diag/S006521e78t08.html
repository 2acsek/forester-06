CHECK ABS WHEEL SPEED SENSOR SIGNAL.

1) Lift up the vehicle.

2) Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

3) Connect the oscilloscope according to the DTC.

4) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

5) Start the wheel, and measure the voltage at the specified frequency.

NOTE:

When this inspection is completed, ABSCM&H/U may record DTC 29.
