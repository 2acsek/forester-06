VEHICLE DYNAMICS CONTROL (VDC) > ABS Sequence Control

SPECIFICATION

1. CONDITIONS FOR COMPLETION OF ABS SEQUENCE CONTROL

When the following conditions develop, the ABS sequence control stops and ABS operation is returned to the normal mode.

1. When the speed of at least one wheel reaches 10 km/h (6 MPH).

2. When the brake pedal is released during sequence control and the stop light switch is OFF.

3. After completion of the sequence control.

4. When a malfunction is detected.
