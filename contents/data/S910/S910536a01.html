ADJUSTMENT

1. ALIGNMENT OF HEIGHT BETWEEN SUNROOF LID AND ROOF PANEL

Loosen the sunroof lid nuts, and then adjust the height by adding (max: four pieces) or extracting (min: zero pieces) shims (normally two pieces) which are installed between the sunroof lid and the roof panel.

Difference of height between sunroof lid and roof panel:

2.0±1.0 mm (0.079±0.039 in)
