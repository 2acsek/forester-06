SUNROOF/T-TOP/CONVERTIBLE TOP (SUNROOF) > Sunroof Assembly

INSTALLATION

Install in the reverse order of removal.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to snag the harness.

NOTE:

• Be sure to connect the harness connector.

• When installing the drain tube, insert it securely into drain pipe.

Length A:

15 mm (0.59 in) or more
