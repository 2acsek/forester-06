FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4DO) > Fuel Filter

REMOVAL

WARNING:

Place “NO OPEN FLAMES” signs near the working area.

CAUTION:

Be careful not to spill fuel.

NOTE:

• The fuel filter is built in to the fuel pump assembly.

• The fuel filter and pressure regulator are one piece and is not to be disassembled.

(A)

Pressure regulator

1. Release the fuel pressure.

2. Remove the fuel pump assembly.

3. Remove the fuel pump.

4. Separate the fuel pump and fuel filter.
