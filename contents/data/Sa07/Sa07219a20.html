REPLACEMENT

1. MANUAL TRANSMISSION

1. Drain the gear oil by removing drain plug.

CAUTION:

• Before starting work, cool off the transmission gear oil well.

• If transmission gear oil adheres to the exhaust pipe, wipe it off completely.

2. Replace the gasket with new part, and then tighten the drain plug to specified torque.

Tightening torque:

Aluminum gasket

44 N·m (4.5 kgf-m, 33 ft-lb)

Copper gasket

70 N·m (7.1 kgf-m, 52 ft-lb)

3. Fill the transmission gear oil through the oil level gauge hole up to the upper point of level gauge.

Recommended gear oil:

CAUTION:

• Each oil manufacturer has its base oil and additives. Thus, do not mix two or more brands.

• If the transmission gear oil is spilt over exhaust pipe, wipe it off using cloth.

Gear oil capacity:

Non-turbo model

4.0 L (4.2 US qt, 3.5 Imp qt)

Turbo model

Without oil pump

3.5 L (3.7 US qt, 3.1 Imp qt)

With oil pump

3.9 L (4.1 US qt, 3.4 Imp qt)
