CAUTION:

(2)

Clamp

4. Disconnect the brake pipes from the PHV.

CAUTION:

• Do not let brake fluid come into contact with the painted surface of the vehicle body. Completely wash away with water immediately and wipe off if it was accidental.

• Use a pipe wrench so that the hex head of the flare nut is not damaged.

5. Separate the PHV from side frame, along with the support.

CAUTION:

When removing the PHV, take great care that no foreign objects enter inside.
