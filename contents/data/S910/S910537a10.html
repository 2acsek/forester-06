SUNROOF/T-TOP/CONVERTIBLE TOP (SUNROOF) > Sunroof Motor

INSPECTION

1. WINDOW AUTO-REVERSE MECHANISM

1. Open the sunroof lid.

2. Forcefully push the sunroof lid in the opening direction while auto-closing, to check whether the window auto-reverse mechanism functions.

3. Check whether the window auto-reverse mechanism operates and the roof moves back 150 mm (5.91 in) in the opening direction.

CAUTION:

Do not place objects in the way to check the auto-reverse mechanism.

NOTE:

• The auto-function is cancelled when the window auto-reverse mechanism is activated more than five times.

• If the auto function is cancelled, reset it.
