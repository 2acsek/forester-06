WIPER AND WASHER SYSTEMS > Wiper Control Relay

INSPECTION

1. Disconnect the connector from the wiper control relay.

2. Connect the positive (+) probe from the battery to terminal 8 and the negative (−) probe to terminal 6. Connect the positive (+) probe from the voltage meter to terminal 3 and the negative (−) probe to the ground.

3. Measure the voltage when the wiper relay operates.

(A)

Switch position

(B)

Voltage

(C)

ON

(D)

12 V

(E)

0 V

(F)

9±2 sec.

If operation is not as specified, replace the switch.
