STARTING/CHARGING SYSTEMS(H4SO) > Generator

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from battery.

2. Remove the collector cover. (Turbo model)

3. Disconnect the connector and terminal from generator.

• Non-turbo model

• Turbo model

4. Remove the V-belt covers. (Non-turbo model)

5. Remove the front side V-belts.

or

6. Remove the bolts on the bracket, and remove the generator.
