GLASS/WINDOWS/MIRRORS > Rear Gate Glass

REMOVAL

1. Remove the rear gate garnish.

2. Disconnect the electrical connectors from rear defogger terminal.

3. Remove the glass in the same procedure as for windshield glass.

NOTE:

The locating pin is adhered to the corner portion on the vehicle’s indoor side of the glass. Use the piano wire to cut each pin.
