AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION(4AT) > Center Differential Carrier

REMOVAL

1. Remove the transmission assembly from vehicle body.

2. Remove the rear wheel speed sensor, and separate the extension case from transmission case.

3. Extract the rear drive shaft.

4. Using the special tools, pull out the center differential carrier assembly.

ST1    499737100 PULLER
ST2    899524100 PULLER SET

5. Pull out the shim(s) from transmission case.
