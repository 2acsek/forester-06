FRONT SUSPENSION > Sub Frame

INSPECTION

1. Check that there is no damage or distortion of the sub frame.

2. Check that the bolts are tightened with the specified torque. If there is looseness, tighten to the specified torque.

Tightening torque:

T1: 55 N·m (5.6 kgf-m, 41 ft-lb)

T2: 71 N·m (7.2 kgf-m, 52 ft-lb)

(1)

M10 × 30 bolts

(2)

M12 × 87 bolts (with wax)

(3)

M12 × 65 bolts (with wax)

(4)

M10 × 90 bolts
