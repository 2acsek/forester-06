INSTALLATION

CAUTION:

• When disconnecting/connecting hoses, do not apply an excessive force to them. After installing, check that no torsion or excessive tension applied to the hoses.

• Seal the disconnected hose with a plug or vinyl tape to prevent dirt from entering.

• Replace the O-rings on the hoses or pipes with new parts, and then apply compressor oil.

1. Install in the reverse order of removal.

2. Charge refrigerant.
