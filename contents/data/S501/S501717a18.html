CONTROL SYSTEMS > Drive Select Cable

REMOVAL

1. Remove the drive select lever.

2. Remove the intake duct.

3. Remove the air cleaner case.

4. Remove the snap pin and clevis pin.

5. Loosen the nut, and disconnect the cable from the cable bracket.

Snap pin

(B)

6. Disconnect the cable from transmission clamp.

7. Remove the bushing & insulator.

8. Remove the cable from underside of the vehicle.
