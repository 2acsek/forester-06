CRUISE CONTROL SYSTEM (DIAGNOSTICS) > Diagnostic Procedure with Diagnostic Trouble Code (DTC)

DTC 32

Detected when the speed is out of the operational vehicle speed range of the system.

Increase the vehicle speed to the speed available for cruise set, and perform cruise set operation again. If DTC is detected even after performing the cruise control setting again, perform the diagnosis of DTC 22.

Refer to DTC 22 for the diagnostic procedure.
