AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION(4AT) > ATF Cooler (With Warmer Function)

INSTALLATION

1. Install the ATF cooler (with warmer feature) to the transmission.

NOTE:

Insert the tip of the ATF cooler bracket to the hole of the torque converter case.

Tightening torque:

T1: 21 N·m (2.1 kgf-m, 15.5 ft-lb)

T2: 23 N·m (2.3 kgf-m, 17.0 ft-lb)

T3: 33 N·m (3.4 kgf-m, 25 ft-lb)

2. Connect the engine coolant outlet hose to the upper part of the engine, and connect the engine coolant inlet hose to the cylinder block.

(A)

Engine coolant outlet hose

(B)

Engine coolant inlet hose

3. Connect the hose to the pipe of the transmission.

(A)

ATF inlet hose

(B)

ATF outlet hose

4. Attach the harness to the bracket.

5. Install the air intake chamber.

6. Fill engine coolant.
