INTAKE (INDUCTION)(H4SO) > Resonator Chamber

REMOVAL

1. Set the vehicle on a lift.

2. Remove the air intake duct.

3. Remove the air cleaner case.

4. Remove the resonator chamber attaching nut on the right side of the engine room.

5. Remove the front wheel RH, and lift the vehicle.

6. Remove the front mudguard RH.

7. Remove the resonator chamber from the inside front fender.
