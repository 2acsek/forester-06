MECHANICAL(H4SO) > Valve Rocker Assembly

DISASSEMBLY

1. Remove the bolts which secure the rocker shaft.

2. Pull out the rocker shaft. Remove the valve rocker arm, spring, and shaft support from the rocker shaft.

NOTE:

Keep all the removed parts in order for re-installing in their original positions.

3. Remove the nut and adjusting screw from the valve rocker.
