MANUAL TRANSMISSION AND DIFFERENTIAL(5MT) > Main Shaft Assembly for Single-range

DISASSEMBLY

1. Put vinyl tape around main shaft spline to protect the oil seal from damage. Then pull out the oil seal and needle bearing by hand.

2. Remove the lock nut from transmission main shaft assembly.

NOTE:

Flatten the lock nut tab before removing the lock nut.

ST1    498937000 TRANSMISSION HOLDER
ST2    499987003 SOCKET WRENCH (35)

3. Remove the 5th hub & sleeve No. 2, baulk lever, 5th baulk ring, 5th drive gear and 5th needle bearing from the transmission main shaft assembly.

(A)

5th hub & sleeve No. 2

(B)

5th baulk ring

(C)

5th drive gear

4. Using ST1 and ST2, remove the rest of parts.

NOTE:

If necessary, use the new sleeve gear & hub assembly, when replacing the sleeve or hub assembly. Because these must engage at the specified point, do not disassemble. If it must be disassembled, mark the engaging point on the spline beforehand.
