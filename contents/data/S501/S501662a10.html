CONTROL SYSTEMS > AT Shift Lock Control System

INSPECTION

1. SHIFT LOCK OPERATION

STEPCHECKYESNO

1.CHECK SHIFT LOCK.

1) Turn the ignition switch to ON.

2) Move the select lever to “P” range.

While brake pedal is not depressed, can the select lever move from “P” range to other ranges?

Inspect “SELECT LEVER CANNOT BE SHIFT LOCKED”.

2.CHECK SHIFT LOCK.

While brake pedal is depressed, can select lever move from “P” range to other ranges?

Inspect “SELECT LEVER CANNOT BE SHIFTED”.

3.CHECK KEY INTERLOCK.

When the select lever is set to other than “P” range, can the ignition switch turned to the “LOCK” position?

Inspect “KEY INTERLOCK DOES NOT BE LOCK OR UNLOCK”.

4.CHECK KEY INTERLOCK.

When the select lever is in the “P” range, can the ignition switch be turned to the “LOCK” position?

AT shift lock control system is normal.

Inspect “KEY INTERLOCK DOES NOT BE LOCK OR UNLOCK”.

2. AT SHIFT LOCK CONTROL UNIT POWER AND GROUND CIRCUIT

WIRING DIAGRAM:

STEPCHECKYESNO

1.CHECK FUSE.

Remove the fuses No. 4, 6 and 18.

Are the fuses No. 4, 6 and 18 blown?

Replace the fuses No. 4, 6 and 18. If the replaced fuse No. 4, 6 or 18 blows out easily, repair the short circuit of harness between the fuse and AT shift lock control unit.

2.CHECK HARNESS CONNECTOR BETWEEN AT SHIFT LOCK CONTROL UNIT AND CHASSIS GROUND.

1) Turn the ignition switch to OFF.

2) Measure the resistance of the harness between the AT shift lock control unit and chassis ground.

Connector & terminal

LHD model

(B373) No. 10 — Chassis ground:

RHD model

(B376) No. 10 — Chassis ground:

Is the resistance less than 1 Ω?

Repair open circuit of harness between the AT shift lock control unit and chassis ground.

3.CHECK BATTERY POWER SUPPLY.

Connector & terminal

RHD model

(B376) No. 3 (+) — Chassis ground (−):

Is the voltage 9 V or more?

Repair open circuit of the harness between battery and AT shift lock control unit, and any poor contact in connectors.
