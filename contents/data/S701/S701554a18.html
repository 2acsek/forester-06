HVAC SYSTEM (HEATER, VENTILATOR AND A/C) > Heater Core

REMOVAL

1. Remove the heater and cooling unit.

2. Open the heater core pipe cover.

3. Loosen the screws to remove the mode actuator.

4. Loosen the screws to remove the foot duct.

5. Loosen the screws to remove evaporator cover.

6. Loosen the screws to remove lower case.

7. Remove the heater core.
