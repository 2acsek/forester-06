FUEL INJECTION (FUEL SYSTEMS)(H4SO) > Tumble Generator Valve Actuator

REMOVAL

1. RH SIDE

1. Release the fuel pressure.

2. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

3. Open the fuel filler flap lid, and remove the fuel filler cap.

4. Remove the intake manifold.

5. Disconnect the connector from the RH tumble generator valve.

6. Remove the RH tumble generator valve.

2. LH SIDE

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Disconnect the connector from the LH tumble generator valve.

3. Remove the LH tumble generator valve.
