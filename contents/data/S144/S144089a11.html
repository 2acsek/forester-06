INTAKE (INDUCTION)(H4DOTC) > Air Cleaner Case

INSTALLATION

Install in the reverse order of removal.

Tightening torque:

33 N·m (3.4 kgf-m, 24.6 ft-lb)

NOTE:

Before installing the air cleaner upper cover, align the protrusion with the hole of air cleaner lower case, and then secure the upper cover and lower case.
