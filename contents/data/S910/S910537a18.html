SUNROOF/T-TOP/CONVERTIBLE TOP (SUNROOF) > Sunroof Motor

REMOVAL

CAUTION:

• When removing the sunroof motor, completely close the sunroof.

• When removing the clip, use great care not to damage the roof trim.

1. Completely close the sunroof lid.

2. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

3. Remove the roof trim.

4. Disconnect the harness connector (A), and then remove the sunroof motor mounting screws (B) and nuts (C).
