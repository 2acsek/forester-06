POWER ASSISTED SYSTEM (POWER STEERING) > Steering Wheel

REMOVAL

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Set the tire to the straight-ahead position.

3. Remove the airbag module.

WARNING:

Always refer to “Airbag System” before performing service on the airbag modules.

4. Place alignment marks on the steering wheel and steering shaft.

(1)

Alignment mark

5. Remove the steering wheel nut, and then draw out the steering wheel from the shaft using a steering puller.
