EMISSION CONTROL (AUX. EMISSION CONTROL DEVICES)(H4DO) > Front Catalytic Converter

REMOVAL

1. DOHC NON-TURBO MODEL

The front catalytic converter is integrated into the front exhaust pipe. Refer to “Front Exhaust Pipe” for removal procedures.

2. TURBO MODEL

1. Remove the center exhaust pipe.

2. Disconnect the front catalytic converter (A) from the rear catalytic converter (B).
