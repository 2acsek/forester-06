ENTERTAINMENT > Rear Accessory Power Supply Socket

REMOVAL

1. FRONT

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the console cover.

3. Disconnect the harness connector, and remove the accessory power supply socket.

2. REAR

1. Disconnect the ground cable from the battery.

2. Remove the rear quarter lower trim.

3. Disconnect the harness connector, and remove the accessory power supply socket.
