WIPER AND WASHER SYSTEMS > Headlight Washer Timer

INSPECTION

1. Remove the headlight beam leveler unit.

2. Connect the positive terminal of voltmeter to headlight washer timer terminal 3, and the negative terminal to terminal 2.

3. Connect the battery positive terminal to headlight washer timer terminals 1, 4, 5, 6, and the negative terminal to terminal 2.

4. Check that the operation is specified below.
