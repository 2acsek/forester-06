PARKING BRAKE > Parking Brake Lever

REMOVAL

1. Lift up the vehicle.

2. Remove the rear wheels.

3. Remove the rear seat cushion.

4. Remove the console box.

5. Loosen the parking cable self-locking nut and console bracket.

6. Disconnect the parking brake switch connector.

7. Remove the parking brake lever.

8. Straighten the tab on the parking brake lever and remove the cable.
