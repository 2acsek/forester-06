EXTERIOR/INTERIOR TRIM > Floor Mat

REMOVAL

1. Remove the front seats.

2. Remove the rear seat cushion.

3. Remove the console box.

4. Remove the side sill front cover, side sill rear cover and center pillar lower trim.

5. Remove the footrest and clip from the floor mat.

6. Remove the mat hook.

7. Remove the mat from toe board area.

8. Remove the mat from rear heater duct.

9. Roll the mat, and then take it out of opened rear door.
