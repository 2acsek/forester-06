EMISSION CONTROL (AUX. EMISSION CONTROL DEVICES)(H4DO) > Rear Catalytic Converter

REMOVAL

1. DOHC NON-TURBO MODEL

The rear catalytic converter is integrated into the center exhaust pipe. Refer to “Center Exhaust Pipe” for removal procedures.

2. TURBO MODEL

1. Remove the center exhaust pipe.

2. Disconnect the rear catalytic converter (B) from the front catalytic converter (A).
