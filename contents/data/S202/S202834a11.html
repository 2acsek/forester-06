FRONT SUSPENSION > Sub Frame

INSTALLATION

Install in the reverse order of removal.

NOTE:

Replace with a new M12 bolt.

Tightening torque:

T1: 55 N·m (5.6 kgf-m, 41 ft-lb)

T2: 71 N·m (7.2 kgf-m, 52 ft-lb)

(1)

M10 × 30 bolts

(2)

M12 × 87 bolts (with wax)

(3)

M12 × 65 bolts (with wax)

(4)

M10 × 90 bolts
